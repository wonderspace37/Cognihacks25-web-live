Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CogniHacks

What is CogniHacks?

CogniHacks is a 12-hour neurotechnology hackathon where participants build innovative brain-computer interface solutions using cutting-edge hardware and AI tools.

Who can participate?

Students, professionals, researchers, and anyone passionate about neurotechnology and AI. No prior BCI experience required!

What equipment do I need?

Just bring your laptop! We provide all BCI hardware, including Emotiv EPOC X, NeuroSky MindWave, and OpenBCI boards.

How do I form a team?

Teams of 2-4 people. You can come with a team or form one on-site. We'll help match you with complementary skills.

What are the prizes?

Over $1,200 in prizes, plus mentorship opportunities, hardware kits, and potential investment connections for winning teams.

What skills should I have?

Basic programming knowledge (Python, JavaScript, or similar) is helpful, but we welcome all skill levels. Curiosity and creativity are most important!

Is food provided?

Yes! We provide breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks throughout the event. Dietary restrictions are accommodated.

Can I work on my own project?

Projects must be started during the hackathon, but you can come with ideas and research. We'll provide project templates and examples.

What if I don't finish my project?

That's completely normal! Judges evaluate based on innovation, technical difficulty, and potential impact, not just completion.

Are there workshops or tutorials?

Yes! We offer BCI basics, AI integration, and hardware setup workshops. Mentors are available throughout the event.

What's the judging criteria?

Innovation (30%), Technical Difficulty (25%), Potential Impact (25%), and Presentation (20%). We value creativity over complexity.

Can I use external APIs or libraries?

Absolutely! Use any open-source libraries, APIs, or tools. Just document what you used and focus on your unique implementation.

What happens after the hackathon?

Winners receive prizes and mentorship. All participants get access to our alumni network, future events, and potential collaboration opportunities.

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes, we maintain a respectful and inclusive environment. Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior is not tolerated.

What if I need to leave early?

While we encourage full participation, we understand emergencies happen. Let organizers know if you need to leave early.

Can I bring guests or spectators?

Due to space and security constraints, only registered participants can attend. However, final presentations are open to the public!

What should I bring?

Laptop, charger, comfortable clothes, and enthusiasm! We provide hardware, food, and workspace. Optional: headphones, power bank, and your favorite snacks.

How do I prepare for CogniHacks?

Brush up on basic programming, explore BCI concepts online, and come with an open mind! We'll provide resources and tutorials during the event.

What makes CogniHacks unique?

We're the only hackathon focused specifically on brain-computer interfaces and neurotechnology. Plus, we provide cutting-edge hardware that's usually expensive to access!
