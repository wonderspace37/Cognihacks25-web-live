Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CogniHacks 2025

Who can participate?

Anyone 18+ with an interest in neurotechnology and AI! No prior BCI experience required. We welcome students, professionals, researchers, and hobbyists.

How much does it cost?

Completely free! Registration, meals, hardware access, and prizes are all included thanks to our generous sponsors.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, chargers, and enthusiasm! We provide all BCI hardware, development boards, WiFi, and power outlets.

Can I work alone?

Absolutely! While teams can have up to 4 members, solo participants are welcome. We'll help you find teammates if you want.
